


Enterprise architecture is the practice of analyzing, designing, planning and implementing EA analysis to successfully execute on business strategies.

EA is especially useful for large businesses going through digital transformation, because it focuses on bringing legacy processes and applications together to form a more seamless environment.

EA is guided by the organization's business requirements - it helps lay out how information, business and technology flow together. A priority for businesses that are trying to keep up with new technologies such as the cloud, IoT, machine learning, and other emerging trends that will prompt digital transformation.

The process is driven by a "comprehensive picture of an entire enterprise from the perspectives of owner, designer and builder.

EA doesn't include a formal documentation structure;instead, it's intended to offer a more holistic view of the enterprise.

Ensuring your EA Strategry has a strong focus on agility and agile adoption.
