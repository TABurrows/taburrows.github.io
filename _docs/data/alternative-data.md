

Often used to feed INVESTMENT DECISIONS, alternative data collates information gained and mined from NON-TRADITIONAL SOURCES.

In the case of Investing, ALTERNATIVE DATA offers Hedge Fund Managers and Institutional Investors information they can not fnd in REGULAR SOURCES like Credit Ratings.

Non-traditional Data Sources include:
- financial transactions
- public records
- SEC Filings
- press releases
- social media monitoring
- online news articles

Once collected and consolidated, this data is mined to obtain unique and timely INSIGHT into a potential investment or business decision.

Alipay allows users to make credit-based purchaes on e-commerce sites run by Alibaba using Alternative Data sources for credit decisions (60% of people do not own a credit card). Some common variables observed in this industry include:
- Make-shift credit scores based on spending habits
- applicants level of education
- frequency of data-input corrections made during the sign-up process
- purchasing and repayment history
- the credit score of the applicant's social contacts

other records include:
- cheating on the national university entrance examination
- public transport convictions


Beyond loans and banking, in real estate a company measuring gentrification has data points including:
- satellite imagery
- online yelp reviews
- online listings
- shipping receipts
- social media posts
[ valuable as real-time data points - no time lags/extended gaps between reporting periods compared to consensus and migration data ]


Other contemporary used-cases for ALTERNATIVE DATA inlcude:
- precision agriculture
- military monitoring
- influenza tracking

eg. farmers rely on drones to analyze time-series images of crops to improve management and yield predictions

Alternative data can be expensive to produce, there is a large amount of ALTERNATIVE DATA that is open data including GOOGLE MAPS and YELP REVIEWS.

In some cases, the HOLDER OF THE DATA doesn't recognize its SECONDARY VALUE and undervalues it or releases it for free.

The complexity of compiling hundred of dis-organize data sources makes it difficult to STORE, MANAGE and ANALYZE ALTERNATIVE DATA using traditional software such as Excel or RDBMs leading to a quiet BREAK-OUT of ALTERNATIVE DATA providers charging Premium Fees to Corporate Clients.
