Security - Phishing

Phishing - hide or secure as many of the pieces of identity as possible. Creation of cloned websites then presenting a user with a scenario.

OAuth Phishing - a type of Credential Phishing that takes advantage of the OAuth standard to gain access to user accounts without needing to know the user's password. It exploits the trust relationship users have with well-known online service providers, as well as trust relationship those providers have with their own third-party applications. It is accomplished by tricking users into granting persistent access to their accounts. OAuth phishing attacks do not use the typical approach used in email phishing (spoofed URL link, sign-in request or attached file). That makes this type of phishing more difficult for a less experienced user to detect and may produce a higher rate of success for the hacker.
