## The four DORA metrics
*Deployment Frequency*: Measures how often an organization successfully releases code to production. 
*Lead Time for Changes*: The time it takes from when code is committed to when it is successfully deployed to production. 
*Change Failure Rate*: The percentage of deployments to production that result in a failure. 
*Mean Time to Recover (MTTR)*: How long it takes for a team to restore service after a production failure. 



## How they are used
*Measure performance*: DORA metrics provide an objective way to assess how well a team is performing in terms of speed and stability. 
*Drive improvement*: By tracking these metrics, teams can make data-driven decisions to improve their software delivery processes. 
*Balance speed and stability*: They are designed to be used together to ensure that teams don't sacrifice reliability for the sake of speed, or vice versa.
